## Summary

Phase 67 wires the `ADAADInnovationEngine` substrate into the live `ConstitutionalEvolutionLoop`. Vision Mode forecast and Mutation Personality are injected pre-proposal at CEL Step 4. Governance Plugins are evaluated post-gate at Step 10. Self-Reflection is emitted on a 100-epoch cadence at Step 14. All injection points are fail-safe — any wiring failure falls back gracefully and CEL-ORDER-0 is preserved.
