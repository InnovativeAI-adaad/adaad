# ADAAD 4.9.0 Release Notes — Phase 24: Health-Driven Review Pressure Adaptation

**Release date:** 2026-03-09
**Baseline:** v4.8.0 · PR-23-REL · 2,867+ tests passing
**Phase:** 24 — Health-Driven Review Pressure Adaptation

---

## Summary

Phase 24 is the first phase that acts on the unified governance health signal
(Phases 21–23) — not by granting any agent new authority, but by surfacing an
advisory recommendation: when governance health degrades, the system should
compensate with proportionally higher reviewer counts.

---

## Validated guarantees (in-repo)

- `HealthPressureAdaptor.compute(health_score)` → `PressureAdjustment`:
  three pressure bands (none/elevated/critical); `advisory_only: True` structural
  invariant; `adjustment_digest` is deterministic SHA-256.
- Elevated pressure (`0.60 ≤ h < 0.80`): `standard.min_count +1`, `critical.min_count +1`.
- Critical pressure (`h < 0.60`): `standard.min_count +1`, `critical.min_count +1`,
  `governance.min_count +1`.
- `low` tier never adjusted; all proposed `min_count` capped at `max_count` and
  floored at `CONSTITUTIONAL_FLOOR_MIN_REVIEWERS`.
- `GET /governance/review-pressure`: bearer-auth-gated, read-only; returns full
  `PressureAdjustment` including `proposed_tier_config` for all four tiers.
- `GET /governance/health` gains `review_pressure` field (additive, non-breaking).
- `GovernanceGate` untouched; all output advisory only.
- 48 new tests; total: 2,915+.

## Pressure adjustment model

| Health band | h range | Pressure tier | Adjusted tiers |
|---|---|---|---|
| Green | h ≥ 0.80 | none | — |
| Amber | 0.60 ≤ h < 0.80 | elevated | standard, critical |
| Red | h < 0.60 | critical | standard, critical, governance |
