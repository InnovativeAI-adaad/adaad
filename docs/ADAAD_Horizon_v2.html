Architecture Delta

Five core layers transform across the 18-month horizon. Here's exactly what changes, what gets replaced, and what gets added.

Current State — Phase 0 / 1
Today
Manual Meta-Controller
Operator triggers agent cycles by hand. No automated mode switching. Scheduling is ad hoc and session-bound.
Flat Agent Registry
Agents versioned as files with no cross-generation linkage. Lineage partially tracked through commit history only.
Static Scoring Rubric
Binary pass/fail only. No weighted fitness functions. Criteria defined manually per sprint cycle.
Manual-Only Governance
All approvals, constraint definitions, and releases are human-executed. No automated evidence collection.
Pydroid3 Sandbox
Android-only runtime. Pure Python. Proof-of-concept execution only. No CI pipeline.

Automated Explore/Exploit switching with human-gated release checkpoints. Every mode transition is logged and auditable.

Adaptive Fitness Functions
Weighted multi-axis scoring: stability, traceability, mutation efficiency. Self-adjusting within human-defined parameter bounds.

Hybrid Governance Engine
Automated evidence binding, cryptographic audit chains, CI/CD compliance checks. Human review at generation — not mutation — boundaries.

Governance Architecture

Three interlocking pillars ensure every autonomous action is safe, auditable, and reversible — at every phase.

🔒
Constraint Layer
What the system can and cannot do
  • ✓Human-defined mutation bounds — zero autonomous override permitted at any level.
  • ✓Android/Pydroid3 compatibility preserved as a hard floor across all phases.
  • ✓Goal contracts are versioned and signed before every phase transition.
  • ✓Release authority is exclusively human at all autonomy levels up to L4.
  • ✓Constraint violations trigger immediate halt and automatic rollback.
📋
Audit Engine
The immutable record of everything
  • ✓Every mutation writes a signed log entry with agent ID, parent ID, score, and decision rationale.
  • ✓Audit logs are append-only and cryptographically chained to commit-bound evidence.
  • ✓Every decision must be deterministically replayable from its log entry.
  • ✓CI/CD validates audit chain integrity on every push. Broken chains block releases.
  • ✓Key rotation events are logged as first-class audit entries — never silent.
👁️
Human Oversight Protocol
Who reviews what, and when
  • ✓Autonomy level increases require a formal milestone: demonstrated stability over N generations.
  • ✓Per-mutation approval at L1. Per-generation approval from L3 onward.
  • ✓Outlier mutations flagged for mandatory human review regardless of autonomy level.
  • ✓Governance mode pauses all autonomous operations instantly — no quorum required.
  • ✓Any designated operator may invoke rollback immediately, at any time.

Risk Register

Six forecasted risks with severity ratings, root cause mechanisms, and concrete mitigations — not vague disclaimers.

Risk Severity Likelihood How It Happens How We Prevent It
Lineage Graph Corruption High Medium Concurrent writes or failed rollbacks leave dangling parent references, breaking traceability. Write-ahead log with atomic commits. Nightly integrity checks. Automated repair with mandatory human sign-off.
Scoring Rubric Drift High Medium Adaptive fitness functions converge on a local optimum that scores well but degrades artifact quality in unmeasured dimensions. Human-defined floor metrics. Adversarial test suites run periodically. Manual rubric audit at every generation boundary.
Exploit Mode Lock-in Medium High Meta-controller biases toward Exploit, eliminating breadth search and causing architectural stagnation over generations. Mandatory Explore floor (≥20%) enforced by governance. Mode balance reviewed at every generation checkpoint.
Android Runtime Regression Medium Low Dependency drift or a mutation silently introduces a C-extension requirement, breaking Pydroid3 compatibility. Dependency manifest validation in CI. Android-emulated replay in every pipeline run. Fail-fast on any extension import.
Audit Chain Tampering High Low Accidental or deliberate modification of historical logs invalidates traceability and compliance posture entirely. Cryptographic chaining of log entries. Append-only storage enforcement. External hash anchoring at every generation boundary.
Operator Review Fatigue Medium Medium High mutation volume at L1/L2 creates approval bottlenecks; operators rubber-stamp without genuine review. Anomaly-first queue surfacing only high-variance mutations. Digest summaries for batch approvals. Daily mutation cap per operator.

Strategic Thesis

ADAAD does not automate trust — it earns it. Every generation of agents that passes governance review extends the envelope of what the system is permitted to do autonomously. Autonomy is the reward for demonstrated reliability, not a premise we begin with.
— ADAAD Design Principle · InnovativeAI-adaad · Forecast Q1 2026 → Q2 2027
North Star Metric
Useful mutation rate — not raw volume. Ten high-quality, traceable, human-approved improvements per generation outperforms one hundred unverified ones, every time.
Non-Negotiable Floor
Full reversibility at all autonomy levels. If a mutation cannot be cleanly rolled back, it does not enter production — regardless of fitness score or human approval status.
18-Month Conviction
By Q2 2027, ADAAD's lineage graph becomes the canonical system of record for every released artifact — a living, auditable genome of the software it has built.
